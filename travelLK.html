About Us

Discover more land with us

travelLK, established in 2016, is a leading travel guide company dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences for foreigners visiting the beautiful island of Sri Lanka. With a passion for showcasing the rich cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and warm hospitality of Sri Lanka, we take pride in curating personalized and immersive travel journeys. Our team of experienced and knowledgeable guides is committed to ensuring that every traveler discovers the hidden gems, cultural wonders, and natural beauty that make Sri Lanka a unique and enchanting destination.

Popular Packages

Sigiriya.

Embark on a journey to Sigiriya, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient rock fortress. Dominating the lush landscape, this iconic site offers a glimpse into Sri Lanka's rich history and architectural marvels. Be prepared to ascend through the breathtaking Lion's Gate and discover the mesmerizing frescoes that adorn the ancient rock walls.

Colombo to Badulla Train Ride:

Experience the enchanting Colombo to Badulla train ride, an exhilarating journey through picturesque landscapes and tea plantations. This scenic route takes you on a captivating odyssey, providing glimpses of Sri Lanka's verdant hills, cascading waterfalls, and charming villages. It's a train ride that promises an unforgettable adventure for any traveler.

$250-$450

Ella Rock

Nestled in the heart of the Hill Country, Ella Rock beckons with its panoramic vistas and invigorating trek. As you ascend, be prepared for sweeping views of tea plantations and misty valleys. Ella Rock is a hiker's paradise, offering a rewarding journey that culminates in a breathtaking vantage point that captures the essence of Sri Lanka's natural beauty.

$250-$450
Book now

Kandy

Immerse yourself in the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, Kandy, where ancient traditions blend seamlessly with modern life. Explore the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, stroll through the vibrant marketplaces, and witness a traditional Kandyan dance performance. Kandy's charm lies in its rich heritage and the serene beauty of the Kandy Lake that graces its heart.

$250-$450
Book now

Trincomalee

Discover the untouched beauty of Trincomalee, a coastal gem with pristine beaches and historic landmarks. Unwind on the golden shores of Nilaveli or explore the historic Koneswaram Temple, perched on a cliff overlooking the azure waters. Trincomalee invites you to indulge in relaxation and exploration amid its tranquil landscapes.

$250-$450
Book now

Anuradhapura

Step back in time as you explore the ancient city of Anuradhapura, a UNESCO-listed archaeological marvel. Wander through the sacred grounds of ancient stupas, dagobas, and monasteries that narrate tales of Sri Lanka's illustrious past. Anuradhapura is a pilgrimage for history enthusiasts, offering a captivating glimpse into the island's cultural and religious heritage.

$250-$450
