About us

our story

Established in 2011, TravelLK has been at the forefront of providing exceptional travel experiences for foreigners exploring the enchanting island of Sri Lanka. With a passion for showcasing the rich tapestry of this tropical paradise, we have dedicated ourselves to curating memorable adventures, allowing travelers to immerse themselves in the diverse culture, breathtaking landscapes, and warm hospitality that define Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

At TravelLK, our mission is simple yet profound: to make your journey through Sri Lanka an unforgettable odyssey. We understand the unique desires and expectations of our foreign guests, and we strive to exceed them at every turn. Whether you seek cultural immersion, thrilling adventures, or tranquil retreats, we are here to craft personalized itineraries that cater to your every need.

Why Choose TravelLK?

  1. Expertise: Our team boasts extensive knowledge of Sri Lanka's hidden gems, ensuring you experience the best the island has to offer.
  2. Tailored Experiences: No two travelers are the same, and neither should their journeys be. We specialize in crafting bespoke itineraries to suit your preferences and interests.
  3. Local Insight: As a team of locals, we bring an insider's perspective to your travels, unveiling the heart and soul of Sri Lanka that goes beyond the typical tourist trails.
  4. Safety First: Your safety is our top priority. We collaborate with reliable partners and adhere to the highest safety standards to ensure a secure and worry-free exploration.

Our Services

  1. Accommodation: From luxury resorts to charming guesthouses, we offer a range of accommodation options that cater to different budgets and tastes.
  2. Transportation: Seamless and comfortable travel is key to a memorable trip. Our transportation services include reliable transfers, private vehicles, and expert local guides.
  3. Guided Tours: Immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry of Sri Lanka with our knowledgeable guides who breathe life into every story and historical site.
  4. Adventure Packages: For the thrill-seekers, our adventure packages promise adrenaline-pumping activities set against the backdrop of Sri Lanka's stunning natural beauty.
